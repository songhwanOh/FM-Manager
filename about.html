About the Site and Its Creator

Welcome to My Favorite Game website! My name is Steven, and I am the creator of this site. As an avid gamer, I have always been passionate about video games. One game that holds a special place in my heart is Football Manager, which is the inspiration behind this website.

My goal with this website is to share my love for Football Manager and provide a platform for gamers to come together and discuss their experiences with the game. Whether you are a long-time fan or a new player, I hope you find valuable information and engaging content here.

I have put in a lot of effort to curate the content and design of this website, and I want to acknowledge the resources and references that I have used in its creation. All text, media, and other resources used on this site are cited appropriately to give credit to the original creators.
