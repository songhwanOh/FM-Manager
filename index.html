Experience the Ultimate Football Management Simulation

Welcome to Football Manager, the world's most immersive and authentic football management simulation game. Step into the shoes of a football team manager and take charge of your favorite club to lead them to glory.


In Football Manager, you have complete control over all aspects of the club's operations. Make strategic decisions on team selection, tactics, transfers, training, and more as you aim to build a winning team and achieve greatness in the footballing world.

Experience the pressure of match days as you watch your team play in realistic 3D or text mode, making in-game adjustments to outwit the opposition. Off the pitch, navigate the challenges of media interactions, contract negotiations, and financial management to keep the club thriving.

Will you steer your team to domestic and international glory, becoming a legendary football manager in the process? The fate of your club is in your hands.
